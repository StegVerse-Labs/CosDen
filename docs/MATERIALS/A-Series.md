# A-Series: Whitening Gels (BrightCore™ Family)

## Overview
The A-Series provides peroxide-based whitening gels optimized for:
- High cosmetic effectiveness
- Low sensitivity
- Compatibility with CosDen optical/mineral layers

A-Series is designed for supervised/at-home cosmetic whitening (not diagnosis or treatment).

---

## Products

### A1 BrightCore™
**Goal:** Core whitening for 10–14 day cycles.

- Carbamide peroxide ~12%
- Nano-hydroxyapatite (nHAP) ~6%
- Nano-silica ~2%
- CPP-ACP ~1.2%
- Potassium nitrate 4%
- Neutral pH (6.6–7.0)
- Daily tray wear: 30–45 minutes

**Use Case:** First-line cosmetic whitening with emphasis on comfort and enamel support.

---

### A2 BrightCore+ Accelerated™
**Goal:** Faster whitening in ~7–10 days.

- Carbamide peroxide ~16%
- nHAP ~8%
- Nano-silica ~2%
- CPP-ACP ~1.2–1.5%
- Potassium nitrate 5%

**Use Case:** Users who want visible results sooner and can tolerate slightly more intensity.

---

### A3 OptiBoost™
**Goal:** Optical + mild whitening (more about brightness than bleaching).

- Carbamide peroxide ~7%
- nHAP ~10%
- Nano-silica ~3%
- CPP-ACP ~2%
- High focus on optical scattering and translucency control

**Use Case:** Maintenance cycles and users with sensitivity, focused on brightness and gloss.

---

### A4 HyperWhite™ (Future Concept)
**Goal:** Maximal cosmetic whitening within CosDen safety framework.

- Multi-phase peroxide release
- Enhanced scatter particles
- Designed for professional supervision

**Use Case:** Flagship “max whiteness” cosmetic protocol (future development).
